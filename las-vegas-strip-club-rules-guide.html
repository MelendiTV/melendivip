Las Vegas Strip Club Rules Guide

Age Requirements

One of the biggest differences between Las Vegas strip clubs is the age requirement.

Many clubs are 21+ because they sell alcohol, while some venues operate as 18+ clubs.

For example, some visitors consider venues like Diamond Cabaret when they are looking for an 18+ nightlife option.

Full Nude vs Topless Clubs

Another major difference is whether a club is full nude or topless.

Full nude clubs often follow different rules regarding alcohol service compared to topless clubs.

Understanding this difference helps visitors choose the nightlife experience they prefer while exploring Las Vegas.

Alcohol Rules

Most Las Vegas strip clubs sell alcohol and operate as full nightlife venues.

However, some venues do not sell alcohol due to local regulations.

Visitors should understand these differences when planning their night.
